# KERNL — Deployment Guide
## How to get your app live on the internet for free

This guide takes you from zero to a live website in about 15 minutes.
No coding experience needed — just follow each step in order.

---

## What you need
- A free GitHub account (github.com)
- A free Vercel account (vercel.com)
- Your Anthropic API key (from console.anthropic.com)

---

## STEP 1 — Put your files on GitHub

GitHub is where your code lives. Think of it as Dropbox for app files.

1. Go to **github.com** and sign in (or create a free account)
2. Click the **+** button (top right) → **New repository**
3. Name it: `kernl`
4. Make sure it's set to **Public**
5. Click **Create repository**
6. On the next page, click **uploading an existing file**
7. Drag and drop ALL the files from this folder into the upload area.
   You need to keep the folder structure exactly like this:

```
kernl/
├── vercel.json
├── package.json
├── api/
│   └── summarise.js
└── public/
    ├── index.html
    └── app.js
```

   Upload them maintaining this structure (GitHub lets you drag whole folders)
8. Click **Commit changes**

---

## STEP 2 — Deploy to Vercel

Vercel is free hosting that runs your app on the internet.

1. Go to **vercel.com** and sign up using your GitHub account
   (Click "Continue with GitHub" — this links the two accounts)
2. Once logged in, click **Add New** → **Project**
3. You'll see your GitHub repositories listed — click **Import** next to `kernl`
4. On the configuration screen, leave everything as default
5. Click **Deploy**
6. Wait about 60 seconds — Vercel builds and deploys your app
7. You'll see a success screen with a URL like: `kernl.vercel.app`

Your app is now live! But it won't work yet — you need to add your API key.

---

## STEP 3 — Add your Anthropic API key (the secret sauce)

This is what lets the app generate summaries. The key is stored securely
on Vercel's servers — it never appears in your code or the browser.

1. Go to **console.anthropic.com**
2. Click **API Keys** in the left sidebar
3. Click **Create Key**, give it a name like "KERNL"
4. Copy the key (it starts with `sk-ant-...`) — save it somewhere safe,
   you can only see it once

5. Go back to **vercel.com** → your `kernl` project
6. Click **Settings** (top navigation)
7. Click **Environment Variables** (left sidebar)
8. Click **Add New**:
   - Name: `ANTHROPIC_API_KEY`
   - Value: paste your `sk-ant-...` key here
   - Leave environment as **All**
9. Click **Save**

10. Go to **Deployments** → click the three dots on your latest deployment
    → click **Redeploy** (this restarts the app with your new key)

---

## STEP 4 — Use your app!

Go to your Vercel URL (e.g. `kernl.vercel.app`) — type a book title,
hit Summarise, and it should work perfectly.

Save the URL as a bookmark or home screen shortcut on your iPhone.

---

## Your free limits on Vercel

Vercel's free "Hobby" plan gives you:
- Unlimited deployments
- 100GB bandwidth per month
- Serverless functions: 100,000 executions per month

That's enough for hundreds of summaries per month easily.

The only cost you'll pay is Anthropic API usage — roughly $0.01–0.03
per summary generated. Summaries loaded from the archive are free.

---

## To update the app in future

1. Edit the files on your computer
2. Go to GitHub → your kernl repository → click the file → edit
3. Vercel automatically redeploys within 60 seconds

---

## Your app URL

Once deployed, share it with anyone. It will be something like:
`https://kernl.vercel.app`

You can also set a custom domain (e.g. kernl.app) from the Vercel
Settings → Domains page — domain registration costs about £10/year.
